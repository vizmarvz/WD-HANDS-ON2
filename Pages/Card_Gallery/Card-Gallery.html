PEPPERMINT BUTLER

occasionally known as the "Dark One", and post-juicing as Pepper Mint is a recurring character on Adventure Time. He is an inhabitant of the Candy Kingdom who is a master occultist, and a loyal butler to Princess Bubblegum.

MARCELINE

a fun-loving and playful 1,000-year-old vampire queen. Unlike a traditional vampire, Marceline does not need to drink blood to survive; rather, she consumes the color red, or other similar tints.

BMO

a sentient video game console and multi-functional robot with a childlike personality and endearing innocence, BMO serves as a loyal friend to Finn and Jake, often providing comic relief and valuable support through its various capabilities.

GUNTER

is the quirky and mischievous pet penguin of the Ice King. Though appearing cute and harmless, Gunter often displays rebellious and sinister tendencies, adding a layer of mystery to his character.

HUNSON ABADEER

also known as the Lord of Evil, is a powerful and malevolent demon from the animated series "Adventure Time." He is the ruler of the Nightosphere and the estranged father of Marceline the Vampire Queen, possessing dark magic and a ruthless personality.

ICE PRINCE

is Ice King's second creation of life, the first was Neptr. He has a gender-swapped counterpart named, Ice Princess. Ice Prince lives in a ice castle outside of the Ice Kingdom.

THE LITCH

is an ancient, cosmic being embodying pure evil and destruction. He is a powerful necromancer with a skeletal appearance, driven by a singular goal to extinguish all life in the universe. His presence is marked by a chilling aura and a relentless pursuit of annihilation.

EARL OF LEMONGRAB

Created by Princess Bubblegum, Lemongrab is known for his strict and tyrannical personality, often exclaiming "UNACCEPTABLE!". He rules over the Lemon Kingdom and is characterized by his erratic behavior, extreme sensitivity, and a penchant for melodrama.

GOLB

was a malevolent entity who is the embodiment of chaos and disorder. He inhabits an unknown dimension and served as the overarching antagonist of Adventure Time and major character in its spin-off series Fionna & Cake.
